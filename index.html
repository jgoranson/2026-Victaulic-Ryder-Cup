Tournament Structure

Format & Rules

Overview

  • 32 players total
  • 8 teams of 4
  • Each team splits into 2 pairs per round
  • Pairs join with pairs from other teams
  • Foursomes rotate each round
  • Team of 4 is the scoring unit
  • Lowest total after 3 rounds wins
Round1

Individual Play — Adjusted Score

July 26  ·  12:00 PM  ·  1st Tee Off
  • All players play their own ball
  • Add all 4 players’ scores, then divide by 2
  • Result is your Round 1 team score
Round2

2-Person Scramble

July 27  ·  9:00 AM  ·  1st Tee Off
  • Each pair plays a scramble format
  • Each pair records one score
  • Pair A + Pair B = Round 2 team score
Round3

Alternate Shot

July 27  ·  2:30 PM  ·  1st Tee Off
  • Each pair plays alternate shot
  • Each pair records one score
  • Pair A + Pair B = Round 3 team score
